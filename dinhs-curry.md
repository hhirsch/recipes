# Dinhs Curry

## Ingredients
- Tofu
- Onions
- Salt 
- Pepper
- Cumin
- Kurkuma
- Crushed Coriander Seed
- Coconut Milk
## Directions
- Dice Tofu
- Fry Tofu
- Remove Tofu from pan and put aside
- Dice Onions
- Fry onions in oil
- Add 2 teaspoons cumin
- Add 2 teaspoons crushed coriander seed
- Add 1 teaspoon of kurkuma
- Fry for 1 minute
- Add coconut milk
- Add salt and pepper
- Add Tofu
- Optionally add fish-sauce or no-fish-sauce
- Serve with rice
